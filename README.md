# Privacy Policy – Bioaesthetics bot
**Effective date:** February 23, 2026  
**Business:** BioAesthetics 305 (Miami, FL)  
**Support / Privacy contact:** stemdoc305@gmail.com

Bioaesthetics bot (“the App”) is an automation tool used to help BioAesthetics 305 respond to Instagram Direct Messages (DMs). This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed, why, and the choices available to you.

---

## 1) What the App does
When someone sends a DM to BioAesthetics 305 on Instagram, the App:
- receives the message via Meta/Instagram webhooks,
- generates a reply using an AI service,
- sends the reply back through Meta/Instagram messaging APIs.

---

## 2) Information the App processes
To operate, the App may process:
- **Message content:** the text you send in Instagram DMs (and limited context needed to reply).
- **Message metadata:** timestamps, thread/conversation identifiers, webhook event data.
- **User/account identifiers:** the Instagram-scoped sender ID and the clinic’s Instagram professional account identifiers provided by Meta.
- **Technical information (limited):** information required to deliver the service reliably (e.g., request/response status, error codes).  

**We do not sell personal information.**

---

## 3) How we use the information
We process information only to:
- respond to your inquiries about BioAesthetics 305 services,
- improve reliability and prevent abuse (e.g., troubleshooting, protecting systems from misuse).

The App is not intended to provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment recommendations.

---

## 4) Sharing / third-party processing
To deliver responses, data may be processed by the following third parties:
- **Meta/Instagram** (to receive and send Instagram messages)
- **OpenAI** (to generate the automated reply)
- **Google (Apps Script infrastructure)** (to run the webhook code)

We share only what is necessary to provide the automated response and operate the service.

---

## 5) Data retention
BioAesthetics 305 does not intentionally maintain a customer message database in this App. The only data retained is the conversation logs that are on the instagram platform and they are not stored elsewhere.
  
Third-party providers (Meta/Instagram, OpenAI, Google) may retain information according to their own policies and legal obligations.

---

## 6) Data deletion requests
You may request deletion of data associated with your interaction that BioAesthetics 305 controls. On our end this would just be deleting our instagram chat from our instagram account

Email **stemdoc305@gmail.com** with:
- Subject: **“Bioaesthetics bot – Data Deletion Request”**
- Your Instagram username and approximate date/time of your message(s)

We may ask for additional information to verify the request and will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## 7) Security
We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect information (for example, HTTPS, encryption in transit and restricted access to systems (the webhook) used to operate the App). 

---

## 8) Children’s privacy
The App is not intended for children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through Instagram messages to the clinic, contact us and we can delete the chat from our instagram dms at **stemdoc305@gmail.com**.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this same URL with a new effective date.
